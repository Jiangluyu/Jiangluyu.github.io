### 词组

##### 与help有关

- **turn to sb. for help** = **seek help from sb.**
- **be always around** = **be available**
- with the help of sb.
- help sb., help sb. with sth., help sb. to do sth.
- offer sb. help, offer help to sb.
- provide sb. with help, provide help for sb.
- **give sb. a hand**
